How it works
Three steps to launch
01

Upload your docs

Drop in PDFs, Word docs, Markdown files, or paste text. DocBot indexes everything automatically.

02

Train in seconds

Our AI reads and understands your content, turning it into a searchable knowledge base ready for chat.

03

Share or embed

Chat inside the app, share a public link, or add the widget to any website with one line of code.

Features
Everything you need

Source citations
